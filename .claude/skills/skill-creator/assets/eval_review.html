Help

How to use this tool:

  • Evaluations Tab: Review test cases from your skill. Edit queries and toggle fields as needed.
  • Trigger Queries Tab: Create queries to test skill triggering accuracy. Mix should-trigger and should-not-trigger cases.
  • Export: When done, click "Export" to download your evaluation set as JSON.

Guidelines:

  • Make trigger queries realistic and substantive (not one-word requests)
  • Include mix of should-trigger and should-not-trigger cases
  • For should-not-trigger, focus on near-misses (queries that share keywords but need different skills)
